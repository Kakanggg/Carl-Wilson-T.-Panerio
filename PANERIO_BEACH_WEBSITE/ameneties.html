Ameneties

Inclusions for Overnight and Day-Tour guests:

  • Free use of common kitchen with stove, grill and refrigerator. No corkage fees on food and beverages except alcoholic drinks (50 Php for alcohol per bottle)
  • Free use of tables and chairs
  • Free use of parking area

Dahican Surf Resort Services:

  • Restaurant and Bar (Curfew at 10:00pm, last order 9:00pm, 9:30pm bar and resort will close)
  • Coffee House
  • Refreshment Station
  • Surf Shop
  • Surf and Skim Lessons
  • Bike/Kayak/SUP Board Rentals

Things to take note of:

  • Full capacity rates during Fridays, Saturdays, Sundays and Holidays
  • We only have common CR and shower facilities for ALL types of accommodation
  • Daytour is for walk-in guests only and shall be accepted on a first come, first serve basis
  • Air-con rooms, surf huts and dorm beds come with bedding
  • Our accommodation does not include breakfast and towels
  • Check-In: 2pm, Check-Out: 11am
  • No entrance fee is required for guests who have booked rooms
  • For reservations we kindly require our guests to make an advanced payment in FULL. Payment is to be fulfilled the same day the booking is confirmed. This is part of our measures to minimize contact with staff whenever possible. If no payment has been made within the day, we will release the room to other guest inquiries so please send us a message to ask if the room is still available before making your payment.
