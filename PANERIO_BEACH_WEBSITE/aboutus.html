About Us

Welcome to Dahican Surf Resort, your ultimate beach getaway destination located along the pristine shores of Dahican Beach. Nestled amidst the natural beauty of Mindanao, Philippines, our resort offers an unparalleled experience where relaxation meets adventure.

Our Story

Established in 2008, Dahican Surf Resort was born out of a passion for the ocean and a desire to share its wonders with the world. Founded by a group of surf enthusiasts and nature lovers, our resort began as a humble beachfront getaway and has since evolved into a premier destination for travelers from across the globe.

Our Philosophy

At Dahican Surf Resort, we believe in the transformative power of nature and the importance of preserving our environment for future generations. Our resort is committed to eco-friendly practices, from our sustainable building materials to our energy-efficient operations. We actively support local conservation efforts and engage with the community to promote environmental awareness and education.

Indulge your taste buds with delicious local cuisine at our onsite restaurant, where fresh seafood and tropical flavors take center stage. Take advantage of our surf lessons and equipment rentals to catch some waves or embark on a thrilling adventure with our guided tours and excursions.

Join Us

Whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, or a little bit of both, Dahican Surf Resort welcomes you with open arms. Come experience the magic of Dahican Beach and create memories that will last a lifetime. Book your stay with us today and let the journey begin!
